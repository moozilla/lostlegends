Battle commands:

ITEM - opens the character's inventory. An item may be selected here for use, such as a weapon.

DEFEND - the character takes a defensive stance, negating all damage that turn.

REST - the character takes a moment to rest, recovering 8 - 12 HP that turn.

MELEE - delivers a raw attack without the aid of a weapon. Useful when weapons are ineffective against foes, or no weapons are in stock.
